# Case Study: OmdaFit — High-Performance Fitness Architecture

## 1. Project Overview
**OmdaFit** is a premium, elite-tier fitness application designed for the Middle Eastern market. The goal was to create a "raw" and "aggressive" digital experience that motivates high-performance athletes through a minimal yet powerful visual language.

## 2. The Challenge
Most fitness apps are either overly clinical (white/blue) or too colorful. The challenge was to build a brand identity that feels like a "dark mode by default" elite gym — focusing on discipline, strength, and intensity.

## 3. Visual Identity & Brand System
- **Deep Obsidian (#000000):** Used to eliminate distractions and create a focused environment.
- **Crimson Red (#FF1E1E):** A psychological trigger for energy and urgency. Reserved strictly for CTAs and progress.
- **Typography:** Cairo (Arabic-first). Bold weights for hierarchy, ensuring readability even during high-intensity workouts.

## 4. UX Strategy
- **Minimalist Friction:** The Onboarding Wizard is designed to be completed in under 60 seconds.
- **Arabic-First Design:** RTL (Right-to-Left) optimization is baked into the grid system, not added as an afterthought.
- **Context-Aware Dashboard:** Centralized progress gauge that adapts based on the 90-day transformation cycle.

## 5. Design System (8pt Grid)
Every component (Cards, Buttons, Inputs) follows a strict 8pt spacing rule to ensure perfect visual balance and scale across all Android/iOS devices.
- **Corners:** 16px (Standard) to 24px (Large Cards) for a modern, premium feel.
- **Contrast:** AAA compliance for text over obsidian backgrounds.

## 6. Key Screens Built
1. **Onboarding:** High-contrast split layout for immediate "Alpha" brand positioning.
2. **Onboarding Wizard:** Step-by-step profile building with crimson progress tracking.
3. **Command Dashboard:** A "Control Center" for the athlete's day.
4. **Workout Engine:** Distraction-free vertical carousel with video integration.
5. **Premium Tiers:** High-conversion pricing cards with "Glow" states for the recommended plan.
6. **Results Gallery:** Social-proof engine using Before/After comparison logic.
